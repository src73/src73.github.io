Patio @ Green Street Smoked Meats

Steven's going away party will be on the patio at Green Street Smoked Meats in the West Loop. I'll arrive around 4:30 PM.

Parking here is a pain. For folks coming from Elmhurst, I'd recommend parking at the Elmhurst Metra Stop and taking the 4:13 PM or 5:13 PM train. Then, ride 30 min to Chicago and walk 10 minutes to the venue. Returning trains leave Chicago at 7:00 PM, 7:40 PM, and 8:40 PM. As a reminder, one can drink their own alcohol on Metra.

Vegetarian food here is a pain, but they never struck me as the sort of place to be picky about folks nibbling on a salad from a nearby place. Alternatively, it's co-located with High Five Ramen and their vegetarian ramen.
